Project Proposal

What is your website about?

  • My website will be about fishing for giant bluefin tuna off the coast of New England.

What is the objective of your website?

  • The website will be instructional with the purpose of teaching anglers how to get started in the fishery.

Who is your target audience?

  • My target audience is saltwater anglers in New England who are not experienced targeting bluefin tuna but want to try it out.

What Content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

  • The homepage will be an overview of the bluefin tuna fishery. It will include some basic information about the fish itself, how to target them and the many reasons that people choose to do so.
  • The first page will contain detailed information of the bluefin tuna and general information on locations and time of year. Information such as the fish's size, feeding habits, migration patterns and where and when to find them will be on this page.
  • The second page will discuss the laws and regulations of the bluefin tuna fishery. It will cover the different licenses to choose from and rules that must be followed.
  • The third page will provide information on the equipment and gear needed to target this species. Everything from safetey gear needed on your boat to specific hooks and fishing line needed will be on this page. There are a lot of aspects to this topic and it might require more than one page.
  • The fourth page will go over how to actually fish for bluefin tuna: different fishing techniques and how to execute them successfully.
