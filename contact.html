About the Author

William Caso has grown up on the south shore of Massachusetts for more than 20 years and has been fishing since he was a little kid. He recently got into fishing for bluefin tuna and has learned a lot from local fisherman. This wesite is dedicated to passing that information along to the anglers of New England who are interested in getting started in bluefin tuna fishing.
